HOW IT WORKS

1. Easy Installation

In less than 60 minutes, Syntigence installs small WiFi chips in strategic places of your business.

2. Data Collection

The WiFi chips collect the MAC address of devices belonging to people walking around in your business. This address is securily sent to the cloud.

3. Database

The collected MAC address is immediately anonymized and stored for maximum 24 hours in the cloud.

4. Processing

The data are processed and analyzed, resulting in insights about the foot traffic and behavior of your customers in your business.

5. Visualization

The results of the data analysis are visualized on an easy-to-understand dashboard tailored to your needs. Heat maps enable you to identify foot traffic in your business.

6. Machine Learning

Our Machine Learning algorithms process and analyze these data to get additional insight company performance, marketing efficiency and operational optimization.
